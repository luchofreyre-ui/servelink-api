## What This Is

Cloudy glass versus etched glass compares removable haze—films and minerals—with permanent surface damage that does not change with surfactants alone.

## Why It Happens

Coatings can fail into a cloudy sheet that mimics etching; oils can mimic both until removed systematically.

## What People Do Wrong

People pick one hypothesis and buy aggressive products that push film cases into true etch cases.

## Professional Method

Run a structured removal ladder on a test corner, document results at consistent lighting, and stop when improvement plateaus.

## Data and Benchmarks

If oil pull with a dry cloth changes appearance materially, you are not finished diagnosing films.

## Professional Insights

Record whether cloudiness is interior, exterior, or interlayer for windows—each has different implications.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when test results are ambiguous across large commercial facades or expensive shower enclosures.
