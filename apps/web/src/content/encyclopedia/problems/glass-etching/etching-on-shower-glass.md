## What This Is

Etching on shower glass is a widespread cloud that survives correct scale removal and feels slightly rough or grabby compared with unaffected glass.

## Why It Happens

Consumer acids, low ventilation, and squeegee neglect create cycles of mineral bond and aggressive removal attempts.

## What People Do Wrong

People confuse etching with coating failure on factory-treated glass—testing methodology differs.

## Professional Method

Remove true soils first, assess clarity, then decide if specialty polishing or panel replacement is the economical path.

## Data and Benchmarks

If only the lower third is affected, splash and pooling geometry usually explains the pattern.

## Professional Insights

Maintenance after restoration must shift to milder prevention—otherwise etching returns on replacement panels.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when coatings are advertised, when big panels need safe handling, or when remodel scope is near.
