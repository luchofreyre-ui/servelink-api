## What This Is

When glass cannot be restored by cleaning is the decision point after correct film and mineral removal still leaves unacceptable scatter or distortion.

## Why It Happens

Coating delamination, deep etch, and interlayer fog in IGUs are common non-cleaning outcomes.

## What People Do Wrong

People keep buying stronger bottles until frames and stone suffer collateral damage.

## Professional Method

Establish a test patch standard, document clarity outcomes, compare replacement quote to restoration scope, and preserve adjacent finishes during any last attempts.

## Data and Benchmarks

Safety tempering limits some polishing approaches—verify before mechanical correction.

## Professional Insights

Property managers benefit from photo standards to align resident expectations.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional for measurement, tempered glass spec, installation, and stone protection during swaps.
