## What This Is

Tracked-in soil on floors concentrates in the first meters past entries, garage connectors, and kitchen transitions, often as darkened grout and dull lanes.

## Why It Happens

Wet seasons, landscaping projects, and pet paws amplify particulate transfer before HVAC filters ever see it.

## What People Do Wrong

People small-mat at wide openings, skip vacuum before wet mopping, or cross wet soil into dry zones on shoes.

## Professional Method

Vacuum dry first, spot-treat gum mud, use measured wet process with rinse discipline, and right-size walk-off mats with cleaning SOPs.

## Data and Benchmarks

Soil color often matches local substrate—red clay versus black asphalt tells a useful story.

## Professional Insights

Pivot points at island stools show secondary tracked patterns beyond the front door.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when commercial entries need scheduled maintenance contracts or when stone needs impregnator reassessment.
