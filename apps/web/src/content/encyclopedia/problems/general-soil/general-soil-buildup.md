## What This Is

General soil buildup is the broad, low-contrast dulling on floors, counters, and fixtures from combined foot transfer, hand oils, and airborne particulate.

## Why It Happens

Soil loads rise with occupancy, pets, outdoor access, and cooking styles; humidity can make films feel tackier sooner.

## What People Do Wrong

People escalate to strong chemistry before improving pickup, or they clean visible centers while ignoring edges where soil actually concentrates.

## Professional Method

Use progressive cleaning: dry soil removal, compatible wet process with good pickup, and targeted detail on high-contact points.

## Data and Benchmarks

Soil recurrence curves help set cadence; entry systems flatten the curve more than kitchen tricks alone.

## Professional Insights

Door hardware and switch plates are soil truth-tellers for whole-home hygiene perception.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when move-in/move-out loads, commercial traffic, or stone programs need extraction equipment.
