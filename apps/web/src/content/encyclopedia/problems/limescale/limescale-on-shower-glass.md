## What This Is

Limescale on shower glass shows as spotty or sheet white deposits that feel slightly gritty and resist quick squeegee passes.

## Why It Happens

Heat, overspray from body products, and irregular squeegee habits leave droplets that dry to minerals.

## What People Do Wrong

People scrub dry powder that scratches, use razor on coated glass, or stack incompatible products.

## Professional Method

Test a corner for mineral versus fatty film, choose the matching sequence, use controlled dwell, then rinse and dry to reveal true clarity.

## Data and Benchmarks

Squeegeeing starts the prevention clock after restoration—maintenance beats heroic removal.

## Professional Insights

Lower door zones often show heavier pattern from bounce-back spray.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when coatings are unknown, when etching is suspected, or when large panels need safe access systems.
