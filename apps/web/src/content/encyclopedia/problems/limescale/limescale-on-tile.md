## What This Is

Limescale on tile accumulates at grout shoulders, texture lows, and shelf edges where water stands briefly before evaporation.

## Why It Happens

Porcelain is often more forgiving than cementitious grout, which can absorb and wick minerals.

## What People Do Wrong

People flood walls, let acids run uncontrolled onto marble thresholds, or confuse efflorescence with tap-water scale indoors.

## Professional Method

Detail horizontal ledges first, control vertical runs with saturated towels as dams, rinse progressively, and verify grout color stability on a test joint.

## Data and Benchmarks

If deposits return quickly after cleaning, evaporation points or slow leaks need review—not only chemistry.

## Professional Insights

Fan runtime and squeegee discipline change mineral deposition rates measurably.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when natural stone is mixed into the assembly, or when waterproofing concerns appear at failing corners.
