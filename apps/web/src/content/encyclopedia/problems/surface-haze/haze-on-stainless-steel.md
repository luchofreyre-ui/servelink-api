## What This Is

Haze on stainless steel reads as a large matte island or overall cloud that reduces specular highlight sharpness compared with a clean neighbor panel.

## Why It Happens

Cooking aerosols, cleaner polymers, and hard-water drying on hot metal can bond a thin scattering layer into brushed valleys.

## What People Do Wrong

People use coarse scrubbers that unify scratches into a cloudy appearance, or they heat panels with oven cleaner overspray risks.

## Professional Method

Cool the surface, remove dry soil, apply minimal compatible chemistry, work with the grain in long passes, and finish with dry buffing; reassess before any abrasive restoration.

## Data and Benchmarks

If haze tracks exactly to cleaner brand changes, film probability is high; if it follows heat discoloration bands, involve OEM guidance.

## Professional Insights

Horizontal hood tops often show the worst haze because dust settles into tacky films between cleans.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when welded commercial hood systems, coated finishes, or warranty service limits homeowner products.
