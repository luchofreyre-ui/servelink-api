## What This Is

Dust returning quickly describes surfaces that look freshly soiled within a short window after an otherwise acceptable clean.

## Why It Happens

Open windows during pollen events, cheap filter bypass, pet dander, and cleaner films that tackify all increase redeposition.

## What People Do Wrong

People blame the cleaner brand while ignoring HVAC maintenance, missing door mats, or using oily polishes.

## Professional Method

Address filtration and entryway capture, remove tacky films if present, and schedule dusting to follow vacuuming so airborne fines resettle before final passes.

## Data and Benchmarks

If dust reload improves after filter upgrade, source control was the lever—not more spray.

## Professional Insights

New textiles and rugs shed for weeks; expect temporary high fiber counts.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when duct assessment, commercial MAT programs, or post-construction HEPA plans are needed.
