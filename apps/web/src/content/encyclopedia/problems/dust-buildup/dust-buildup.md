## What This Is

Dust buildup is visible particulate and fine film on horizontal surfaces, electronics, trim tops, and fan blades where air slows and lets particles settle.

## Why It Happens

HVAC filtration, textiles, pets, and outdoor ingress continuously generate dust; static on dry finishes attracts fines after wiping.

## What People Do Wrong

People dry-dust with charged synthetics that skip crevices, or they wet-wipe first and create mud lines in grooves.

## Professional Method

Dry removal with appropriate tools for the texture, damp detailing for capture, and then source checks (filters, textiles, entryway mats) stabilize appearance between visits.

## Data and Benchmarks

Dust reload rate correlates with foot traffic lanes, pet paths, and window usage—not only cleaning frequency.

## Professional Insights

Ceiling fan leading edges often predict whole-room dust load; they are fast diagnostic surfaces.

## When to Call a Professional

Call a professional when post-construction mineral dust, large atriums, or commercial HEPA programs are required.
